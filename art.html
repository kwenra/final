Faces of Seoul

5/13/16 | 5:30PM

Charting her journey to Korea after living for many years in the United States, filmmaker Gina Kim composes a breathtakingly candid and intimate portrait of the ever-changing city. Praised as a “palimpsest of time” by Seoul National University Professor Min Eun Kyung, Faces of Seoul combines both original and archival footage, including video taken by Kim herself of the infamous Sampoong Department Store collapse in 1995.
