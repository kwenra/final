Must Go Events for the Aspiring St. Louis Artist

Watch others make art and make art yourself! Dancers, actors, artists, writers, all are welcome!

A compilation of events that all aspiring artists are welcome to watch or even participate in!

DANCE

Enjoy watching other people dance? Or wanting to find a dance opportunity to participate in? Well you've come to the right place!

Whatever it be, whether watching a performance or even participating in a performance, we're sure to have something up to your taste right here!

MUSIC

If your idea of a great night out is listening to live music/talkshows, then this is the place for you!

Delve into this compilation of musical events in a variety of locations and with a variety of genres to cater to everyone!

ART

Want to get your hands out on things and working on amazing projects? The art article is here to fulfill your needs!

Find a bunch of hand-on events that cater to every whimsical craft possible! Go explore new mediums!

FASHION

Work work fashion baby.

Get ready for a glamorous night out with high fashion on both yourself and on other people!
